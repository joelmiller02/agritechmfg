RCP-12 Cover Crop Crimper

❮
❯

The RCP-12 is a robust 12-foot 3-point mounted cover crop crimper designed specifically for orchards. Built with heavy-duty steel construction, it provides reliable termination of cover crops while maintaining a simple, durable design.

Features:
  • 12FT Chevron Pattern Drum
  • 300LBS Per Foot Weight (Including Water)
  • Category 2 3-Point Connection
  • 7" Square Tube Welded Frame
  • Powder Coated Finish
