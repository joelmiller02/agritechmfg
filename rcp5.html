RCP-5 Cover Crop Crimper

❮
❯

The RCP-5 is a compact 5-foot 3-point mounted cover crop crimper designed specifically for vineyards. Built with heavy-duty steel construction, it provides reliable termination of cover crops while maintaining a simple, durable design.

Features:
  • 5FT Chevron Pattern Drum
  • 310LBS Per Foot Weight (Including Water)
  • Category 2 3-Point Connection
  • 6" Square Tube Welded Frame
  • Powder Coated Finish
